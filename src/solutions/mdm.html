Why Your Business Needs A Reliable Alternative To The Plain Old Telephone Service (POTS)?

The plain old telephone service (POTS) has been showing its age for years as its underlying copper-wire infrastructure becomes ever costlier to maintain. Most major telecoms companies are now working to phase out support for these aging analog lines with a view to a complete phaseout over the next few years.Despite this, every business still requires the level of reliability that the POTS once offered, and it is essential that they prepare themselves for the upcoming phaseout. Cellular solutions can help bridge that divide by making it possible to stay ahead of government regulations and avoid having to rely on more expensive solutions, such as satellite broadband.

Emergency Systems

The coverage and reliability of the traditional telephone network remains unsurpassed, even by the latest fixed-line broadband solutions. For example, while there’s no denying the benefits of VoIP for everyday business communications, vendors are universally required to state that it should not replace an emergency phone system. Critical functions, such as fire alarms and security systems require a degree of reliability that only the POTS could once accommodate.

Fax Systems

Despite the decline of the fax machine in recent years, many companies still rely on them for receiving orders from customers and vendors. This is especially the case in highly regulated sectors like healthcare, finance, and legal, where many digital solutions still cannot meet the demands of regulatory compliance.

Point-of-Sale Systems

For most companies, point-of-sale systems are a vital lifeline. For example, if something like a card reader goes offline for an extended period, a retailer could lose thousands of dollars’ worth of sales. To prevent these costly disasters, such businesses need a redundant system that works even when there’s a widespread broadband or power outage.

Business Resilience

Spotty internet connections remain a problem in many parts of the country, and outages can happen at any time. For companies that rely entirely on digital communications, such outages simply make it impossible to continue doing business. Extended outages can have severe and long-lasting consequences.

4G LTE Is The Best Alternative

Konnected Concepts helps organizations prepare for the pending phaseout of POTS lines by providing dependable mobile connectivity solutions that don’t rely on the constant availability of the local power grid. With 4G LTE, companies can enjoy the same degree of reliability as that of the trusted POTS infrastructure, albeit with the added benefits of more bandwidth and higher speeds.

4G LTE connectivity is ideally suited to always-on applications, such as security alarms, gates, and point-of-sale systems. When used as a redundant connectivity solution, 4G LTE is perfect for ensuring operations can continue to run smoothly even when there’s a widespread outage. As the traditional POTS infrastructure becomes more expensive, 4G LTE connectivity offers a more cost-effective and dependable solution.
